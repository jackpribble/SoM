====== Leticia ======
**Leticia **is [[Kira|Kira's]] mother and [[Irving|Irving's]] wife. She wears her slightly curly hair cut short, and owing to her past as a hunter, also wears the typical hunter's garb of Sinobi. She now tends to the farms and gardens of Sinobi, but is still adept at handling a bow or spear if need be. She is quite patient and rarely aggressive, often taking a long-term view on solutions.
