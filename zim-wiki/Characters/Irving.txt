====== Irving ======
**Irving** is [[Kira]]'s father and [[Leticia]]'s husband, currently living in the village of [[Locations:Sinobi|Sinobi]]. He wears his hair in a long ponytail and sports a beard that he cuts occasionally, and like many other residents of Sinobi, he wears the classical garb of a hunter. He enjoys telling stories and legends, and has a hearty laugh. He rarely hunts anymore, preferring to instead to handle the instruction of the younger generation.
